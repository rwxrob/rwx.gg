PicoCTF: 2Warm

Can you convert the number 42 (base 10) to binary (base 2)?

What is a Base?

We could just use a binary converter, but let’s actually do the counting, at least for a while.

Base 10 (Decimal) Base 2 (Binary) Base 16 (Hexadecimal)
0 0 0
1 1 1
2 10 2
3 11 3
4 100 4
5 101 5
6 110 6
7 111 7
8 1000 8
9 1001 9
10 1010 a
11 1011 b
12 1100 c
13 1101 d
14 1110 e
15 1111 f
16 10000 10
17 10001 11

Let’s use bc (which is included on all UNIX/Linux systems) to do the conversion.

All we have to do is set the output base (obase) to 2.

$ bc
