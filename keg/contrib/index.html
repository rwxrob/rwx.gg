Donating and Contributing

Helping Out Our Knowledge Development Team

Donating and contributing to RWX.GG can take many forms. Perhaps you want to write content and submit a merge request, maybe you want to help moderate the chat, or perhaps you just want to donate money or other resources to the cause.

Submitting Content

Anyone is welcome to submit content that is relevant to overall course and site goals. Simply submit a pull request or open a ticket with suggestions or bug fixes that are needed. Please be sure to read the style guidelines before attempting a content contribution.

Streaming

Another way to contribute is to do your own streams. Streamers who regularly stream their own interpretation of this content and their working through the course can simple open an issue requesting a link on our streamers page.

Donating

Currently the only way to donate is to subscribe to rwxrob.live or send money directly. This may change in the future as our project grows.
