What is Continuous Integration and Delivery / CICD?

A Way to Constantly Release Something

CI/CD refers to the combined practices of continuous integration and continuous delivery. By automating the quality assurance testing changes can be made instantly and released on a rotational schedule allowing them to be usable sooner. This concept is popular in the technology industry but has made its way into other fast-moving industries as well including knowledge management.
