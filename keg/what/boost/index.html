What is a boost?

A Push to Get You Going

A boost is the thing parents give to their kids when teaching them how to ride a bicycle, a bit of initial momentum to help them get their balance and direction. One you give a boost you get out of the way and let the person moving take over.

The concept of a boost must be fully understood and used when the focus is on autodidactic learning — which stuffy people might prefer to call an alternative pedagogical approach. The priority and responsibility are always with the person doing the learning — not the “teacher” or mentor.
